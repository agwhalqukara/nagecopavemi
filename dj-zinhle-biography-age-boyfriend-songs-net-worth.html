Dj Zinhle Biography, Age, Boyfriend, Songs &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 23, 2024

DJ Zinhle (born 30 December 1983) is a South African deejay who served as the resident DJ on the SABC1 music show Jika Majika.

DJ Zinhle is also an actress, after having appeared on television series like Rhythm City and eKasi. She was born on 30 December 1983, in Dannhauser, KwaZulu Natal, South Africa.

Dj Zinhle Age

She was born on the 30th of December 1983 in Dannhauser, KwaZulu Natal, South Africa. As of 2021, she is 38 years old and celebrates her birthday on December 29th every year.

Dj Zinhle Boyfriend | Dj Zinhle and AKA

She was once in a relationship with Rapper AKA but their relationship came to an end when she was pregnant with their child Kairo to be with Bonang Matheba. Zinhle is believed to be in a relationship with Brendon Naidoo

Dj Zinhle Baby

She is a mother of one beautiful daughter Kairo Owethu Forbes aka Baby K, who came as a result of her past relationship with rapper AKA.

Achievements

  • In 2009, Mail & Guardian nominated her as one of the 300 “Young South African You Have to Take to Lunch”.
  • She is a SAMA 19 song of the year nominee, 12th Metro FM Awards song of the year nominee and a Channel O Awards nominee.
  • In 2014, Bona Magazine named her as one 10 of SA’s Most Influential Women.
  • Oprah Magazine named her as one of 21 Powerful African Women In 2014.
  • In 2016, Channel 24 named as the 4th Most Powerful Celeb in South Africa.
  • She is Glamour Magazine’s Woman of Year for 2016.
  • In 2016, she was ranked as the second MOST INFLUENTIAL YOUNG SOUTH AFRICAN in an annual award and ranking scheme for young South African Achievers between 15-40 years.
  • In 2017, she designed and showcased a fashion line alongside a young designer Chesney Williams at the Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Joburg.
