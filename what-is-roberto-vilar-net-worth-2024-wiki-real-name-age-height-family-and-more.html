Roberto Vilar Full Overview and Wiki

The Journey Begins

Roberto Vilar Fernández, the man who would become a beacon of Galician comedy, embarked on his entertainment voyage in the lush landscapes of Xove, Spain.

Born on July 5, 1971, Roberto’s early life remains shrouded in mystery, but what we do know is that his path to stardom was anything but ordinary.

From Sketches to Stardom

His journey into the limelight began with Os Tonechos, a comedy duo that became a staple on the late-night variety show Luar.

This gig wasn’t just a job; it was the launchpad for Roberto’s ascent in Spanish television.

The segment’s success catapulted him to his own late-night sketch and variety show, Land Rober, marking the beginning of a new era in his career.

The National Spotlight

In 2011, he made a significant leap from the regional network CRTVG to the national stage with Mediaset España.

This transition was monumental, with Roberto taking the helm of Salta a la vista on Cuatro, showcasing his versatile talent to a broader audience.

The move wasn’t just a change of scenery; it was a testament to Vilar’s rising star in the Spanish entertainment industry.
